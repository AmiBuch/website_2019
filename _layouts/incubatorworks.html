What is Incubator Works?

A support line to reach CIE: If a student has an idea and wants to make a startup out of it, Incubator works acts as a student-driven mentoring system to tune your idea before you pitch in front of an actual incubator. We have come up with a pipeline where once a student with an idea approaches us, we help them connect to the right set of people from CIE and/or others from the ecosystem who can provide the student with necessary mentorship, while also providing our best help to the student in their initial days of their startup journey.
